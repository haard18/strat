Polymarket BTC 15-Minute Strategy

Core Rules (Non-Negotiable)

  • Market: BTC – 15 minute only
  • Bet size: $10–$20 max (never increase)
  • Entry timing: ~2 minutes before expiry
  • Probability: ≥ 93% and ≤ 97%
  • Price-to-beat distance: $100 minimum, prefer $150–$200
  • NO trade if last 1-min candle is violent or spiky

Why This Works (When It Works)

  • Late-stage certainty beats prediction
  • Price buffer absorbs small wicks
  • Small size prevents single-trade ruin
  • Fails instantly in high volatility

Profit Assumptions

Risk Reality Check

  • One bad trade can erase 5–8 wins
  • Increasing size breaks the math
  • More trades ≠ more profit
  • Calm markets are mandatory
This is a probabilistic grind, not a get-rich scheme.
The goal is survival first, profit second.
