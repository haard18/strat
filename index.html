BTC 15-Minute Polymarket Income Strategy

Objective

Generate small, repeatable profits from high-confidence BTC 15-minute markets and periodically extract funds rather than compounding risk.

Core Trading Rules

  • Market: BTC – 15 minute only
  • Bet size: $10–$20 per trade
  • Entry window: ~2 minutes before expiry
  • Choose side with ≥ 93% probability (≤ 97%)
  • Price-to-beat distance: $100 minimum (ideal: $150–$200)
  • Skip trades during sudden spikes or unstable candles

Why This Can Produce Income

  • Late-stage probabilities are more reliable
  • Price buffer reduces last-minute noise
  • Small sizing allows many attempts without account stress
  • Focus on one market improves judgment

Capital Management Mindset

  • Withdraw profits regularly
  • Do not scale size quickly
  • Consistency beats aggression
  • The goal is reliability, not hero trades
This strategy is designed to be boring, repeatable, and extractive.
If rules are followed, profits can be slowly pulled out without stressing capital.
